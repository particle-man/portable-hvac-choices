# 3D Models Index — Dual-Hose Conversion Parts

This index catalogues 3D-printable parts for converting single-hose portable AC units to dual-hose operation. Files are hosted on their original platforms (Printables, Thingiverse, MakerWorld). Download STL files from the links below.

---

## De'Longhi Pinguino Series

### Pinguino Intake Adapter (with 150mm Exhaust Adapter)

- **Source:** [Printables #1355319](https://www.printables.com/model/1355319-delonghi-pinguino-intake-adapter-with-exhaust-150m)
- **Author:** TheUnlimited64
- **Tested on:** PAC CN92 Silent
- **Compatible models:** PAC N90, PAC N82, PAC EX112 (and likely other Pinguino models with similar intake layout)
- **Hose size:** 150mm exhaust
- **Print notes:** Requires 250x250mm build plate minimum. Print blue intake part with brim. Grey hose adapter needs no supports. Seal side vents with tape after installation. Keep intake hose ≤1.5m for best airflow.
- **Files:** Intake adapter, hose adapter

### DeLonghi PAC EX105 Dual-Hose Box

- **Source:** [MakerWorld #563047](https://makerworld.com/en/models/563047-delonghi-pac-ex105-ac-unit-2-hose-box)
- **Author:** BimboLab
- **Compatible models:** PAC EX105
- **Description:** Two-hose conversion box with quick-release adapters for both hoses

### Pinguino Exhaust Adapter (125mm Duct)

- **Source:** [Thingiverse #2539991](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2539991)
- **Author:** bjeromin
- **Description:** Compact adapter that fits around the back exhaust on Pinguino series, fits inside 125mm duct

### Midea/Comfee/Trotec/Airwell Dual-Hose Conversion

- **Source:** [Printables #1348047](https://www.printables.com/model/1348047-single-hose-to-dual-hose-ac-diy-airwell-midea-trot)
- **Author:** Dávid Magyari (ItachiOne)
- **Also on:** [Thingiverse #7084679](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:7084679)
- **Tested on:** Airwell MFH012-C41
- **Compatible models:** Midea, Trotec, Comfee (same platform/chassis)
- **Hose size:** 150mm (5.9") with quick-release twist-lock connections
- **Performance:** ~30% improvement in energy consumption vs single-hose
- **Print notes:** Hose openings positioned 30cm apart, angled 45° outward and slightly downward

### Comfee Two-Hose Conversion

- **Source:** [MakerWorld #2261362](https://makerworld.com/en/models/2261362-comfee-mobile-air-conditioner-two-hose-conversion)
- **Description:** Custom intake part fits Comfee grille with bayonet connection for hose
- **Hose size:** 150mm (5.9")
- **Print notes:** PETG recommended, 0.3mm layer height
